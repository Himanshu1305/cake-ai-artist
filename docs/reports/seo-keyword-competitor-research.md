## Top-3 SERP Analysis Per Query

Recurring pattern: **tool/generator pages use plain descriptive titles with "Free," no year/brackets/numbers**; **listicle/blog pages heavily use numbers + "2026."** This split is the single most important structural insight.

BasedLabs & Genbaz **exact-match the keyword including "free online"** and run FAQ+HowTo. Clonable template. SERP also contested by app-store listings. **Our /3d-cake-designer ranks #1-3 here — must keep matching this schema-rich template.**

### "cake design online free"
Canva ("Free and customizable cake templates"), Vondy, BasedLabs, CakeGPT. "Free" appears in nearly every winning title. Dominated by template libraries + reused generator pages. **No dedicated pure-play AI-cake "online free" landing page — opportunity.**

### "photo cake maker"
CapCut ("Online Birthday Cake Maker with Name and Photo"), LightX, MyNamePix, hpbd.name. Splits into two intents: (a) **overlay a photo onto a cake image** (utility sites, WhatsApp/Instagram shareable) and (b) **AI-generate photorealistic cake**. Winning titles stack "Name and Photo." Content gap.

### "birthday cake with name"
India-heavy, dominated by free name-editor utilities (Cakeiy: "Birthday Cake With Name Edit \| Happy Birthday Cake Generator Free \| Cakeiy", MyNamePix, writenamepics, hpbd.name). Titles repeat exact keyword 2–3× + "Free/Edit/Generator/Online." Intent overwhelmingly free/instant/shareable, not purchase.

### "wedding cake designer online"
Weakest/most-fragmented SERP — Quora, app listings, local bakeries, one AI tool (CelebrateAlly: "Free AI Wedding Cake Generator \| Visualize Wedding Cakes"). **Low competitive density = strong opportunity.**

### "cake design ideas 2026"
100% listicle territory (FlowerAura "Top 15 Trending Birthday Cake Designs in 2026", THE WED "10 Major Cake Trends for 2026"). The ONLY query type where **numbers + "2026" dominate titles.** Blog play → internally link to generator.

4. Blog/listicle pages: number + "2026" mandatory (opposite of tool pages).

## Seasonal Opportunities (India 2026)

India festival cake demand is huge and owned entirely by **delivery bakeries** (FlowerAura, Winni, Bakingo, FNP), NOT AI design tools. Their formula: "[Festival] Cakes \| Order Online \| Same Day Delivery." **An AI-design angle ("design/visualize your [festival] cake free") is wide open.**

| Occasion | Date 2026 | Keyword targets | Competition | Angle |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Raksha Bandhan / Rakhi** | **Fri Aug 28** | rakhi cake design, rakhi cake for brother/sister, rakhi cake with name | High bakeries / **zero AI tools** | **Nearest — build NOW.** "Rakhi Cake Design Ideas 2026 — Free AI Generator for Brother & Sister." |
| Ganesh Chaturthi | ~Sep | ganpati/modak cake design | Medium | Distinctive shapes; Pinterest gap. |
| Diwali | ~Nov 8–10 | diwali cake design/ideas, diya cake | Med-High | Big seasonal spike. Start early Oct. |
| Christmas | Dec 25 | christmas cake design ideas 2026 | Medium | Global reach. Listicle + generator. |
| New Year | Dec 31 | new year cake design 2027 | Low-Med | Get ahead with a 2027 page. |

**Playbook per festival:** (1) listicle blog ("Top N [Festival] Cake Designs 2026", number+year, ~1,000–1,200 words, one design per H2) + (2) generator landing page ("Free AI [Festival] Cake Generator"). Publish 3–5 weeks before the date. **Rakhi (Aug 28) is the immediate priority.**
