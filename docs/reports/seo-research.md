## The two most important findings
1. **Two title systems.** Tool/generator pages win with plain `[Exact Keyword] — Free … Designer` (NO year/numbers, exact-match keyword in title *and* H1). Blog/listicle pages win with `Top N … 2026` (number + year mandatory). We were mixing these up on blog posts (Phase 1/5 fixes that).
2. **AI citations reward extractable provenance.** Statistics (+30-41%), inline citations (+30-40%), and a 40-60 word direct answer placed in the **top 30% of the page** (55% of AI-Overview citations come from there) are the highest-ROI structural changes (Phase 3/4).
